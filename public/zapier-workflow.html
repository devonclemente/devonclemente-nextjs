Schedule Configuration

The trigger frequency can be customized to meet specific operational requirements. For this demonstration, a daily schedule was selected to optimize the balance between operational costs and system responsiveness while ensuring consistent data processing workflows.

Data Retrieval Configuration

This webhook performs a secure GET request to retrieve data from external APIs or databases. The configuration supports authentication protocols, custom headers, and response formatting to ensure seamless data integration across multiple platforms and services.

AI Processing Engine

The Google AI Studio integration leverages Gemini's advanced language model capabilities to process, analyze, and transform raw data into actionable insights. Custom prompts can be configured to generate reports, summaries, or structured analysis based on specific business requirements.

Email Distribution System

The final step automatically distributes processed reports via Gmail's enterprise API. The system supports dynamic recipient lists, custom formatting, attachment handling, and delivery confirmation to ensure reliable communication of critical business intelligence to stakeholders.
